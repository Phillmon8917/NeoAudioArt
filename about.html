About me

As a Sound Engineer, Music Composer, and Filmmaker, Neo has been perfecting his craft since 2013. He began his journey with music composition, followed by formal studies in Sound Engineering and Music Production at Southwest Media College. Neo's professional experience includes working as a Technical Assistant Producer at Radio Turf, University of Limpopo.

He later pursued an NQF5 qualification in Film and TV Production at Bolobathaba Media Group, where he gained extensive experience in Audio Post-Production. Neo's impressive portfolio includes work on documentaries such as "Always & Otherwise", "Street Snappers", "Hung Out Dry", "Six Kings and Six Queens", and various other documentaries and short films.
